Monster Energy Zero Sugar (review)

Like our friend Esperblox I also read the can description when buying a new monster. This one doesn’t really describe what it tastes like, to describe this flavour. It tastes like every other normal energy drink but more fancy. It really has that cheap energy drink taste but made it fancy. I really like that but there’s another taste in the background that kinda ruins it. It also gives me a tummy ache which is a no no. And yes there’s a difference between zero sugar and sugar monsters. To give it a rating.

4/10


Monster Energy Ultra Ultra Strawberry Dreams (review)

God I love this flavour. It’s sweet, tart and tastes like strawberry’s. It’s definitely one of the best ultra flavours. It’s extremely sweet and tart and those two things bind together perfectly. The only issue I have with it is that it could’ve had more taste and strawberries. Anyhow this flavour is so good. Definitely of my favourites.

8.5/10


Monster Energy Juiced Khaotic (Review)

Oooh khaotic I love this flavour. It’s really sweet and has a weird flavour that I just love. I can’t really describe what it tastes like, maybe a mix of different fruits, it really is chaotic (haha get it?). Anyhow this is one of my favourite monster flavours out there. The only problem I have with it is that’s it’s not sugar free, sugary drinks make me feel weird and make my teeth hurt, so minus a point for that. Overall the rating I would give this would be

9/10
